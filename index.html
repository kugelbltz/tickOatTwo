Tick oaT Two is a game invented by youtuber Oats Jenkins based on TickTacToe. For more information, watch his video HERE

How to play

Tick oaT Two is a two player game where the opponents take turns marking the spaces in a three-by-three grid with vertical and horizontal lines.

If a cell has already been played once, the second player can still play it making a red and blue . However, you are not allowed to play the cell that has just been played.

The goal is to be the first player to complete 3 in a row (horizontal, vertical or diagonal)
